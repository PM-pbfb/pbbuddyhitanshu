    Agent Reassignment Logic ğŸ”„

    If a lead is created and assigned to an agent, and the lead has a talk time of 1 minutes, then:

    • If the assigned agent is unavailable for a particular day (e.g., on leave or a week off), the lead is reassigned to another available agent.
    • Once the original agent returns, the lead is reassigned back to them.
    • However, if the call duration between the new agent and the customer exceeds the call duration between the original agent and the customer, the lead remains with the new agent.

    Calculation of Active Days ğŸ—“ï¸

    • Active Days Count: Only the days when the agent is actively working will be considered. If the agent is not active on a particular day (e.g., on leave or off), that day will not be counted in the scheduling logic.

    Call Schedule for Unconnected Leads ğŸ“†

    If a lead has not been contacted yet, the following call schedule will be followed:

    • Day 1:
      • 4 Calls: The lead should receive a total of 4 calls on the first day.
      • 2 Calls in one shift (e.g., morning shift).
      • 2 Calls in another shift (e.g., evening shift).
    • Day 2:
      • 2 Calls: The lead should receive 2 calls, scheduled in shifts different from those on Day 1.
    • Day 3:
      • 1 Call: The lead should receive 1 call on this day.
    • Day 5:
      • 1 Call: Another 1 call should be made on this day.
    • Day 7:
      • 1 Call: The final call in this sequence should be made on this day.

    Important Notes ğŸ“

    • Application of Call Flow: This call schedule is applicable only if none of the calls have been connected with the lead.
    • Connected Calls: If a call is connected, the scheduling and prioritization of further calls will follow a different set of rules.

    Nullify First Attempt ğŸš«

    • Condition: The last attempt will be nullified if the customer revisits (via Revisit, Inbound, or Email) between the first and second attempt.

    Revisit Criteria ğŸ”™

    • Time Difference: A revisit will only be considered if the time between the last visit and the current visit is greater than 15 minutes.
    • During Call: A revisit during a call will be considered a revisit.
    • Website Revisit: If the customer revisits the PB website within 60 minutes, it will be treated as if the revisit did not occur.

    Active Callback ğŸŸ¢

    • Time Frame: All scheduled callbacks within CB-15 minutes to CB+15 minutes fall under this category.
    • Sorting: Internal sorting will be based on the difference between the current time and the callback time. The nearest callbacks will have higher priority.
    • Handling: If not attempted, the lead will be moved to the â€˜Passive Callbackâ€™ bucket.

    Calling Logics ğŸ—£ï¸

    Manual Attempts rules ğŸ–ï¸

    • If historical talk time (TT) < 2 min and same day TT = 0 and there are 4 unanswered attempts (UA), the advisor cannot release the call and dial manually.
    • Maximum 2 manual attempts allowed in a day if same day talk time < 3 minutes.
    • Maximum 4 manual attempts allowed in a day if same day talk time > 3 minutes.
    • If total talk time on Leadid is less than 1 minute, manual attempts are not allowed and the system will dial according to its priority.

    Burst Calling rule ğŸ’¥

    • At customer level, if more than 2 unanswered attempts occur in 30 minutes, no calls are allowed to that customer for the next 2 hours.
    • Exceptions include: Paymentcallback, Callback (set before Burst calling), CTC, IB and Revisit allowed.
    • If same day talk time > 5 minutes, manual calls are allowed.

    AI<>Calling - Status Stamping ğŸ¤–ğŸ“

    • If we receive any negative sentiment from the customer (Cx), we apply a cool-off period of 1 day.
    • On the second negative sentiment, the cool-off period extends to 3 days.
    • On the third negative sentiment, the Cx is unsubscribed for 30 days.
    • If, during this period, the Cx revisits our journey and opts to resubscribe, they will be subscribed again.
    • If they do not take any action, they will be automatically resubscribed after 30 days.
    • On rejection, we pass an identifier in the sub-status.
